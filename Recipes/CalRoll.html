Did you know?

The California Roll was not invented by the Japanese nor in California as the name might suggest, but in Vancouver, Canada

Ingrediants to use:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • crab
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

step by step guide

  1. take a sheet of nori
  2. spread some rice on the nori
  3. flip the nori
  4. add your toppings
  5. roll the sushi
  6. cut the sushi and serve!
